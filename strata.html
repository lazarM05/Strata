STRATA
loop · layer · build

Strata records a loop of whatever you play, then lets you keep recording new layers on top of it — in time, automatically — so a song builds up one pass at a time.

Headphones strongly recommended — without them, each new layer also picks up everything already playing, and it compounds with every layer you add.
